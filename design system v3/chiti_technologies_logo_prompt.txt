# Chiti Technologies — Master Logo Prompt Guide
*Part of the Chiti Technologies V3 Ecosystem*

## The Philosophical Foundation
In Indian philosophy (specifically Kashmir Shaivism and Vedanta), the word **"Chiti" (चिति)** translates to **Pure Consciousness, Supreme Intelligence, or the Universal Life Force**. It is the fundamental energy that perceives and connects everything.

For "Chiti Technologies", the logo must symbolize the intersection of **ancient cosmic intelligence** and **cutting-edge, premium digital infrastructure**. It should feel alive, intelligent, and precise—matching the V3 Design System’s cinematic aura.

Below are meticulously crafted text prompts designed for Midjourney v6 (or similar high-end image synthesis models) to conceptualize this logo.

## 4. Typography / Font Recommendations (Post-Generation)
When applying text next to the AI-generated logo mark:
- Keep the logomark on the left, text on the right.
- Use the V3 Design System base font for the brand name: **Outfit** (ExtraBold or Black weight) for the word "CHITI".
- Use **Inter** (Regular, widely tracked) for the word "TECHNOLOGIES" below it.
- **Color:** "CHITI" in stark `#F9FAFB` (White), "TECHNOLOGIES" in your secondary `--text-muted` tone to establish visual hierarchy.
